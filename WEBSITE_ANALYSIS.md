## Modern Redesign Strategy

### Design Philosophy
Build a premium, professional security consulting website with:
- Modern, clean typography hierarchy
- Strategic use of whitespace
- Professional color palette (deep navy/charcoal with accent colors)
- High-quality imagery and visual depth
- Clear trust signals and credibility markers
- Smooth interactions and micro-animations
- Mobile-first responsive design

### Content Structure
1. **Hero Section** - Compelling headline, subheading, CTA
2. **Trust Indicators** - Certifications, years of experience, client testimonials
3. **Services Overview** - 4 service cards with icons and descriptions
4. **Detailed Service Sections** - Expanded information on each service
5. **Why Choose Us** - Competitive advantages and differentiators
6. **Team Section** - CEO bio and team credentials
7. **Contact Section** - Contact form and information
8. **Footer** - Links, hours, contact info
